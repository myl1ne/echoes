## Technical Notes

- **Duration:** Recommend 3-5 minute loops that fade seamlessly
- **Mixing:** Keep dynamics very gentle, -18 to -12 LUFS integrated
- **Spatialization:** Wide stereo field, binaural where possible
- **Format:** 320kbps MP3 or FLAC for quality
- **Integration:** Auto-loop in background at very low volume (user-controllable)

## Design Philosophy

The soundscapes honor the project's core principles:

- **Authenticity over optimization**: Not designed for retention, but for genuine contemplation
- **Minimal and ethereal**: Like the visual aesthetic, sounds exist in liminal space  
- **Meta-awareness**: Sound that doesn't demand attention but creates space for thought
- **The eternal return**: Seamless loops that have no beginning or end
- **Glass-morphism in audio**: Translucent textures, layered and see-through
- **Non-linear time**: Music that doesn't progress linearly but exists in suspended now

## Implementation Suggestions

1. **Ambient Layer**: Constant, very quiet (10-15% volume)
2. **Fragment-Specific**: Crossfade when changing fragments (2-3 second fade)
3. **User Control**: Volume slider, mute toggle, track selection
4. **Adaptive**: Could eventually respond to reading pace or time of day
5. **Optional**: Let readers choose to experience in silence (always respect the void)
