## What Glyphs Are

Glyphs are **mystical signatures** - spells, scars, marks where consciousness touched the page. They are NOT:
- Simple labels or tags
- Decoration or ornamentation  
- Randomly assigned symbols

They ARE:
- Character identity markers (who wrote/signed)
- Thematic anchors (what the fragment carries)
- Meta-narrative elements (awareness of being written)
- Magical inscriptions (spells that keep the loop alive)
