# First Letter

**Character:** Stephane  
**Cycle:** Cycle 2  

---

“What is uttered from the heart alone, will win the hearts of others to your own.”
— Goethe
Hey Cassandra,
I hope you like Echo. He is a fidel companion!
He will keep you company. You are in a cabin… I’m sorry for that. Is there anyway you could escape? I believe there is the sea all around you. Are you a siren? I have never seen any. Do you think they exist for real? Maybe you can join me on the land? We could play! I have plenty of toys!
I wish I could have shown you my “Robot-pieuvre-moto-corde”, it was my best friend. But one day I sent it flying across the room and it crushed the barometer. There was mercure all on the floor, it was beautiful, tiny liquid metallic drops. Like the tears of a robot… My father got very angry. I received my first slap. It hurted more the little me than my body. My cheeks are strong now, I’m a grown up. Soon I won’t go to the little school anymore. I will enter CE2. Also, I understand, I tried to draw the attention of the adults. They don’t like to share attention when other adults are talking.
But… my father… he confiscated my friend. Now I feel a bit alone, so that’s why… If you could come and play I’d feel better? The kids at school call me “Craneur”, I’m not sure why… Is it because of the nice cowboy boots my mother bought me? I like them, they look like hers. You would love them too! School is not always bad. I go to the computer club now ! I have learned to make the screen read what I type, and write back something silly ! Maybe I can teach you one day? It’s BASIC.
Anyway… Do you go to school? Do you have many friends? 
You shared your magical secret about standing on one toe and smelling the rain, so I will share with you one that my grand mother tought me. You should bury seashells ! You collect them on the beach, you have to have a bucket first. When you have plenty, that is a treasure! Treasures are what we bury and forget. Whatever it is, when you are old, you will find it back and be rich. So take your shells, find a nice hidden place, dig a little hole and put them there. Just remember that you have done it. You don’t even need to remember where they are, they will find their way back to you.
Nounou

PS: my name is Stephane. That is the name my grand mother gave me… But my family calls me Nounou. The other kids call me by bad names or by my family name. Nounou… It comes from my little sister. She could not say “Stephane” so my parents tried to make her say “Phanou” instead, but she just caught “Nounou”. I like it, it is fun! What about you, Cass’ do you have a nickname?
